How Earthy Hub Works

  1. Collecting Items

    Users collect items to donate from their homes or local areas, preparing them for the donation process.

  2. Inspection

    Once items are collected, they undergo an inspection to ensure they meet the quality standards set by Earthy Hub.

  3. Listing

    All inspected items are then listed on the Earthy Hub platform, allowing potential recipients to view available donations.

  4. Donation to Charity

    Items are donated to partnered charities that specialize in distributing these goods to those in need.

  5. Receipt Submission

    Donors are encouraged to submit a receipt for their donations, which helps track impacts and provides acknowledgment.

  6. Pickup Confirmation

    Finally, once all processes are complete, donors receive confirmation of pickup, ensuring that their items have reached the intended charity.
